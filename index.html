About Me

My name is Stuart Schafer, unless I’m in trouble or did something wrong. In that case, my name is Rudy Gesegnet. I have lived my entire life in the state of North Carolina. I was born in Fayetteville, NC, aka Fayet-nam, Fay-Ruit, and Bragg-dad. I went to school at Appalachian State in Boone, NC, and then UNC Charlotte. I met my wonderful, beautiful, smart, amazing, talented, gorgeous wife in Charlotte, NC, and we were married on my birthday (I better not forget our anniversary!).

I’ve worked in Charlotte for the past 17 years mostly in inside sales, tech support, and customer service. We moved to Cary for a great job opportunity for my wife, and I went back to school to pursue my passion; computer coding. I recently finished school and created many elaborate applications, from front-end to back-end, I can code full-stack applications. Please visit my portfolio for more insight on my creations.
